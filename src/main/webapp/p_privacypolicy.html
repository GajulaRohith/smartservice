Professional's Privacy Policy

At Smart Service, we recognize the importance of privacy for our professionals. This policy outlines how we handle and safeguard the personal and professional information you provide when you work with us. We collect data such as your name, contact details, skills, certifications, and banking information to facilitate payments, manage your account, and ensure you receive suitable job assignments.

Your data helps us match you with appropriate clients, communicate job details, and provide updates about service requests. We also use the information to improve our services and enhance the overall experience for both professionals and clients on our platform.

We are committed to maintaining the security of your data. To protect your information from unauthorized access or misuse, we implement strict security protocols, including encryption, secure servers, and limited access to sensitive data. Only authorized personnel who need the data to perform their roles can access your information.

We do not share your personal information with third parties except when required to fulfill legal obligations or to facilitate the services you offer through our platform. For example, we may share your contact details with clients who book your services to ensure smooth communication and coordination.

We may periodically update this Professional's Privacy Policy to align with new features, technologies, or legal requirements. We recommend reviewing this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect and use your information. By continuing to offer your services through Smart Service, you agree to adhere to the terms set forth in this policy.

Your trust is crucial to us, and we aim to create a safe and secure environment for all professionals working with Smart Service. We are dedicated to providing a reliable platform that values your privacy and supports your professional growth. If you have any questions or need to update your information, please contact our support team.

By choosing to work with us, you agree to the terms outlined in this Professional's Privacy Policy. We strive to uphold the highest standards of data privacy and security, ensuring a trusted and efficient service for professionals associated with Smart Service.
