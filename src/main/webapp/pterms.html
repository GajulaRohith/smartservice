1. PROFESSIONAL AGREEMENTS

A. Scope of Work

Professionals are responsible for delivering services as per the agreed terms with clients. This includes maintaining high standards of quality, professionalism, and punctuality. Services should be executed as described, and any deviations must be communicated promptly. The scope of work ensures that clients receive accurate and consistent service from all professionals associated with Smart Service.

B. Professional Conduct

All professionals are required to exhibit courtesy, respect, and professionalism at all times. It is imperative to maintain client confidentiality and adhere to the ethical standards set by Smart Service. Violations can lead to disciplinary actions, including contract termination. Professionals should keep their skills updated and be ready to adopt best practices within their domain.

C. Compliance with Policies

Professionals must comply with Smart Service's policies and relevant legal requirements. Regular training and updates on policies will be provided to ensure alignment with industry standards. Compliance is essential for continued association with Smart Service, and non-adherence may result in penalties or cessation of contracts.

D. Feedback and Improvement

Feedback from clients is a valuable tool for professional growth. Smart Service encourages professionals to actively seek and respond to feedback. Constructive feedback should be used to improve service quality, and continuous learning is essential for maintaining high standards. Professionals can request support to address any specific feedback or performance issues.

E. Service Quality and Standards

All professionals must adhere to the quality standards established by Smart Service. This includes regular evaluations of service performance and implementing improvements as necessary. Quality assurance measures will be in place to monitor service delivery, ensuring that all clients receive exemplary services consistently.

F. Safety and Compliance

Professionals are responsible for ensuring their own safety and the safety of clients during service delivery. Compliance with local regulations and industry standards related to safety is mandatory. Regular safety training will be provided, and any incidents should be reported immediately to Smart Service management.

G. Professional Development

Smart Service supports the ongoing professional development of its professionals. Opportunities for training and skill enhancement will be provided. Professionals are encouraged to engage in continuous learning to improve their expertise and service offerings, ultimately benefiting their career growth and client satisfaction.

2. ENGAGEMENT TERMS

A. Service Availability

Professionals should ensure availability as per their scheduled commitments. Cancellations or rescheduling must be communicated promptly to avoid inconvenience to clients. Smart Service aims to provide a reliable platform for professionals, and adherence to scheduled appointments is crucial for maintaining client trust.

B. Professional Appearance

Maintaining a professional appearance is essential when representing Smart Service. This includes dressing appropriately and adhering to hygiene standards. Professionals should present themselves in a manner that instills confidence and reflects the brand values of Smart Service.

C. Payment and Invoicing

Payments for services rendered will be processed as per the terms agreed with Smart Service. Accurate and timely invoicing ensures smooth financial transactions. Professionals are expected to maintain transparency in billing and promptly address any discrepancies. Adherence to payment schedules is important for continued collaboration.

D. Cancellation Policy

Professionals must adhere to the cancellation policies set by Smart Service. Last-minute cancellations can disrupt client schedules and affect the brand reputation. Clear communication regarding any changes in service availability is essential to manage expectations and minimize inconvenience.
