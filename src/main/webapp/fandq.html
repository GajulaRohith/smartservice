How do I book a service?
Booking a service with Smart Service is easy. Just sign up,and Log in browse the available services, and click 'Book Service' to get started.
How can I reset my password?
If you've forgotten your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page. You will be prompted to enter your registered email address, and enter your new password
What is Smart Service?
Smart Service is a platform that connects users with trusted service professionals for various home and office needs. From cleaning to maintenance, we make it easy to find and book reliable services.
How does Smart Service work?
Simply sign up, browse our list of services, choose the one you need, and schedule an appointment. A professional will be assigned to you and will arrive at the scheduled time to complete the service.
Are the service professionals verified?
Yes, all our service professionals go through a rigorous background check and verification process to ensure safety and quality service.
What measures are taken to ensure safety during the service?
We prioritize safety by providing our professionals with safety training, and they are equipped with necessary tools and protective gear. Additionally, we verify their backgrounds before they join our platform.

What types of services are available?
We offer a wide range of services including home cleaning, office cleaning, plumbing, electrical repairs, and more. Browse through our services section to find what you need.
Can I book multiple services at once?
Yes, you can book multiple services at once. Simply add the services you need to your booking, and our system will schedule them accordingly.
Do you offer same-day services?
Yes, we do offer same-day services for select categories. Availability may vary, so we recommend booking early to secure your preferred time slot.
Can I customize a service package?
Absolutely! You can customize a service package based on your specific needs. Contact our support team to create a tailored service plan just for you.
Are there any hidden charges?
No, there are no hidden charges. All prices are transparently displayed before you make a booking, and the final bill will reflect only the agreed-upon charges.
Can I get a detailed breakdown of my bill?
Yes, the invoice will include a detailed breakdown of all services and charges, including taxes. You can view and download it from your account dashboard.
What should I do if I am overcharged?
If you believe you have been overcharged, please contact our customer support team within 48 hours of receiving the invoice. We will review your case and resolve any discrepancies.
Can I cancel my service and get a refund?
You can cancel your service up to 24 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. For cancellations made less than 24 hours before, partial charges may apply.
How is my personal information secured?
We prioritize your privacy. Your data is protected through industry-standard encryption, and we do not share your information with third parties without your consent.
Is online payment safe?
Yes, all online transactions are secured using advanced encryption technologies, ensuring the safety of your payment details and preventing unauthorized access.
How do you handle my data after the service is completed?
Your personal data is securely stored, and we only retain it for as long as necessary to provide our services and comply with legal requirements. We follow strict data retention policies to ensure your information remains secure.
Can I delete my account and all associated data?
Yes, you can request the deletion of your account and personal data at any time by contacting our support team. We will process your request in accordance with our data privacy policy.
Do you share my information with service providers?
We only share necessary information with our service providers to facilitate the service you requested. All service providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements to protect your data.
